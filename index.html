The Chocolate Chip Factory

Best Chocolate Chip Cookies in the World

Sharing our best recipe, right here!

Get ready to bake, taste and become adicted to the best chocolate chip cookies you have ever tried in your life.

PREPARATION:
1) Set your oven to 350F
2) Get ingredients out from the pantry and fridge
3) Make coffee (because it makes everything better!)
4) Put on your favorite tunes

INGREDIENTS
  • 1 CUP of Dark Brown Sugar

  • 1/2 CUP of White Sugar

  • 2 EGGS

  • 1/2 CUP Vegetable Oil

  • 1 TBS Vanilla

  • 2 CUPS of ALL Purpose Flour

  • 1/2 tsp of Salt

  • 2 tsp Baking Powder

  • 2 CUPS of Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips

    • INSTRUCTIONS:
    1) Mix sugars, eggs and oil
    2) Add Vanilla - keep mixing!
    3) Carefully add Flour
    4) Add Chocolate Chips! YUM!
